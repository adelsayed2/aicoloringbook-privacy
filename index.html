Who we are

AI Coloring Book ("we", "us", "our") is a mobile application that lets children and families create custom coloring pages using AI image generation. We are operated by Adel Sayed (individual developer), reachable at adel.elmenawy@yahoo.com.

1. Children's privacy (COPPA / GDPR-K)

This app is designed for children aged 2 and up. We comply with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and applicable international equivalents (UK Children's Code, EU GDPR Article 8, UAE PDPL).

  • We do not collect personal information from children without verifiable parental consent.
  • Account creation requires a parent or guardian email address and acknowledgement of this policy.
  • Parents may review, correct, or delete their child's account data at any time by contacting us at adel.elmenawy@yahoo.com.

3. How we use uploaded photos

When a parent enables the "photo-to-coloring-page" feature:

  • The photo is transmitted over an encrypted HTTPS connection directly to our image generation server.
  • The photo is processed in memory only — it is never written to disk, stored in a database, logged, or retained in any form after the API call completes.
  • The resulting coloring-page image is returned to the device. We do not keep a copy.
  • We do not use uploaded photos to train AI models.
  • We do not share uploaded photos with third parties.

4. AI-generated images and SynthID watermarking

Coloring pages are generated using Google's Gemini image generation model running on Google Cloud Vertex AI. Google applies an invisible digital watermark called SynthID to AI-generated images. This watermark:

  • Is embedded in the image data by Google and cannot be removed by us.
  • Does not contain any personal information.
  • Identifies the image as AI-generated if analyzed by compatible tools.

We do not apply any additional tracking or identification mechanisms to generated images.

5. Data we do not collect

  • We do not collect behavioral data for advertising purposes.
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information to any third party.
  • We do not use cross-app tracking or advertising identifiers (no IDFA / GAID usage for ads).
  • We do not run behavioral advertising of any kind.
  • We do not share data with social networks or analytics companies beyond the anonymous, aggregated metrics described in Section 2.

7. Data retention and deletion

  • Account data: Retained until you delete your account. To delete, use Account → Delete Account in the app, or email adel.elmenawy@yahoo.com.
  • Usage events: Auto-deleted after 90 days.
  • Photo data: Not retained (see Section 3).
  • Generated coloring pages: Stored on-device in the app's local gallery only. Uninstalling the app removes all local data. We do not store copies.

8. Security

All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher. Stored credentials (API keys, service account keys) are held in environment-variable secrets and never exposed in source code or client-side bundles.

9. Parental controls

Parents can:

  • Delete the account and all associated data from Account → Delete Account within the app. This immediately and permanently removes all stored data.
  • Contact us at adel.elmenawy@yahoo.com to exercise any COPPA/GDPR rights (data access, correction, deletion). We will respond within 48 hours.

The app's parental gate (a math challenge on every sensitive action) helps ensure children cannot change settings or access mature features without a parent present.
